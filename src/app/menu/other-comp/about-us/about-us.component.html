About Us

MyTeam11 is India’s most trusted fantasy sports app. Launched in 2016, MyTeam11 presently acquires a user base of 18 million+ across the country. Giving online fantasy sports gaming in India a boost, we help you engage with various sports with exciting rewards and winnings. Whether your game is Cricket, Football, Kabaddi or Basketball, MyTeam11 has daily contests and tournament leaderboard leagues you’ll love. You have a vast choice of contest options, from Practice Contests where you can test your skills for free to Mega Contests where users can lay hands on huge winnings.
